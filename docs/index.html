Privacy Policy

Introduction

VocabAce is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information necessary for authentication and functionality. Specifically, the following data is processed:

  • Email Address: Collected and stored via Firebase Authentication for account creation and login purposes.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized data about how you interact with the App, such as features accessed, session duration, and error logs, may be collected to improve performance.
  • Third-Party Services:
    • Firebase Authentication: Used to securely manage user accounts and sign-in credentials, including email addresses.
    • Superwall: Handles in-app purchases and processes minimal purchase-related data for validation.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Provide and improve the App’s features.
  • Secure the App against fraudulent access.
  • Process and manage in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Perform analytics to enhance the user experience.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. All interactions with Firebase and Superwall are encrypted. We do not store sensitive user data beyond authentication credentials handled by Firebase.

Your Privacy Rights

If you wish to delete your account or remove your authentication data, you may do so through the app settings or by contacting us directly at vocabaceapp@gmail.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
